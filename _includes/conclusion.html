So, which aspect does tip the scale?


Which beer aspects are the most important for users?

If we were lazy, we could’ve simply looked at all the numerical ratings of our reviews and conclude – people like all aspects equally and give their grades correspondingly. Luckily, our high school Psychology course finally paid off and our doubt was awakened. With the help of an observational study and users’ opinions, we found out that Taste and Aroma are what they write and care about primarily. So breweries, listen carefully: if you mix the ingredients just right, your work will probably pay off!
